About

Hey there! I'm Majid Gholami. With a Ph.D. in Applied Science and a diploma in data science, I love diving into data science and machine learning to solve real-world problems.

I've worked on some cool projects related to data science and machine learning:

NSERC Postdoctoral Fellow | THE UNIVERSITY OF BRITISH COLUMBIA
JUL 2023 - PRESENT, VANCOUVER, BC

Applied novel machine learning and deep learning techniques for energy management solutions.

Working on developing a digital twin of the UBC Smart Hydrogen Energy District (SHED) at Mérida Labs.

Developing strategies and a digital platform for public transit fleet decarbonization.

Mitacs Postdoctoral Fellow | LAVAL UNIVERSITY
AUG 2022 - MAR 2023, QUEBEC CITY, QC

  • Structured and analyzed the data to define new predictive models for the Canadian sheep market.
  • Built time-series models to predict weekly sales volumes and average prices for Agneau-info: An intelligent platform for the marketing of lamb.
  • Summarized research and analysis in an easy-to-understand and engaging manner, providing insights and recommendations for different stakeholders.

NSERC-Doctoral Researcher | UNIVERSITY OF QUEBEC
JAN 2018 - JUL 2022, QUEBEC CITY, QC

  • Investigated machine learning techniques for the modeling of electrochemical processes in wastewater treatment.
  • Authored multiple journal papers and book chapters, showcasing exceptional analytical and research skills (Google Scholar).
  • Delivered engaging presentations and created visually compelling posters to effectively communicate findings to diverse audiences.

Here are some of the skills I bring to the table: Python, Matlab, SQL, Tableau, Git, NumPy, SciPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, scikit-learn, XGBoost, TensorFlow, PyTorch, NLP, LLM, AWS, Hadoop, Spark.

In my free time, I enjoy fishing, hiking, and photography.

If you'd like to connect or think there's an opportunity that might be a good fit for me, please reach out! I'm always excited about exploring new challenges and collaborating with like-minded people to make a difference.

Projects

EUR-USD Currency Pair Price Prediction

Statistical Analysis of Chicago West Nile Virus

An in-depth statistical analysis of the West Nile Virus in Chicago, including data visualization, interpretation of trends, and statistical modelling.

Natural Language Processing (NLP) of Hotel Reviews

Natural Language Processing project analyzing hotel reviews to extract sentiments and key themes from customer feedback along with sentiment classification using machine learning.
